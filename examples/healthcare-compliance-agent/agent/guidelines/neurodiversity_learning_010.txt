PROTOCOL_TYPE: Neurodevelopmental & Learning
ID: ND_VAR_010
TITLE: Neurodiversity Support (Dyslexia, Dyscalculia, Dyspraxia, Dysgraphia, ADD/ADHD, SPD)

1. SCREENING & DIAGNOSIS
   - Multisensory assessment for Dyslexia/Dysgraphia.
   - Occupational therapy evaluation for Dyspraxia (coordination).
   - Sensory Profile 2 for SPD (Sensory Processing Disorder).

2. THERAPEUTIC INTERVENTIONS
   - Dyslexia: Orton-Gillingham approach (multisensory).
   - ADHD/ADD: Executive function coaching, environmental modification.
   - Dyscalculia: Visual math aids, manipulatives.
   - SPD: Sensory integration therapy, calming soundscapes (Brown/White noise).

3. SAFETY GATES
   - Verify school/work accommodations (IEP/504).
   - Monitor for secondary anxiety/depression.
   - Rule out hearing/vision impairments as primary causes.
