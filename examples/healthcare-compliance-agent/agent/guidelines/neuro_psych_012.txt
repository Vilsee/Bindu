PROTOCOL_TYPE: Neurological & Psychological Disorders
ID: NEURO_PSYCH_012
TITLE: Advanced Behavioral Health (Bipolar, Schizophrenia, Epilepsy, Tic Disorders, Tourette's, OCD)

1. PHARMACOTHERAPY & MONITORING
   - Schizophrenia: Antipsychotic compliance and Metabolic monitoring.
   - Bipolar: Mood stabilizer (Lithium/Valproate) serum levels.
   - Epilepsy: Anti-epileptic drug (AED) adherence; Seizure tracking.
   - OCD: ERP (Exposure and Response Prevention).

2. BEHAVIORAL & SENSORY SESSIONS
   - Tic Disorders/Tourette: CBIT (Comprehensive Behavioral Intervention for Tics).
   - Misophonia: Sound masking (White noise/Pink noise) to reduce trigger impact.

3. SAFETY GATES
   - Seizure safety protocols (First aid training for family).
   - Monitor for "red flag" side effects (Dystonia, Agranulocytosis).
   - OCD: Prevent repetitive ritualization within the agent interface.
