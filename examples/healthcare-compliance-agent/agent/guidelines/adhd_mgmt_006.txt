TITLE: ADHD Evaluation and Management
ID: ADHD_MGMT_006
VERIFICATION: 2024.6

1. STIMULANT THERAPY (First-line):
   - Methylphenidate: Start 5-10 mg twice daily.
   - Amphetamine Salts: Start 5 mg daily or twice daily.

2. NON-STIMULANT OPTIONS:
   - Atomoxetine: Start 40 mg daily.
   - Guanfacine: 1 mg daily at bedtime.

3. SAFETY GATES:
   - Baseline cardiovascular screening (Heart rate/BP).
   - Monitor height/weight in pediatric cases.
   - Screen for history of substance use disorder.
