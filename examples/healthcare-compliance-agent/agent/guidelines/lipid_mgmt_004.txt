TITLE: Hyperlipidemia and Lipid Management
ID: LIPID_MGMT_004
VERIFICATION: 2024.4

1. STATIN THERAPY:
   - High-intensity: Atorvastatin 40-80 mg daily.
   - Moderate-intensity: Atorvastatin 10-20 mg daily or Simvastatin 20-40 mg daily.

2. TARGETS:
   - Primary target: LDL cholesterol reduction.
   - High risk: LDL goal < 70 mg/dL.

3. SAFETY GATES:
   - Baseline Liver Function Tests (LFTs).
   - Monitor for myopathy/muscle pain.
