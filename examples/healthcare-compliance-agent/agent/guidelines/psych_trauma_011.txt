PROTOCOL_TYPE: Psychiatric & Trauma Support
ID: PSYCH_TRAUMA_011
TITLE: Complex Trauma & Dissociative Disorders (PTSD, CPTSD, DID, OSDD, Personality Disorders)

1. CORE MANAGEMENT
   - Trauma-Informed Care (TIC) across all interactions.
   - DID/OSDD: Stabilization and safety planning (Phase 1).
   - CPTSD/PTSD: Grounding techniques, EMDR, or somatic experiencing.
   - Personality Disorders (BPD/ASPD): Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT).

2. GROUNDING & SENSORY SOOTHING
   - Use of Binaural Alpha Waves for emotional regulation.
   - 5-4-3-2-1 sensory grounding exercise.
   - Safe space visualization.

3. SAFETY GATES
   - CRITICAL: Immediate suicide risk assessment (SI/HI).
   - Monitor for "switching" or high dissociative states.
   - Ensure clinician has specialized training in DID/OSDD.
