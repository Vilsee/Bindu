TITLE: Major Depressive Disorder First-line Therapy
ID: DEPRESSION_005
VERIFICATION: 2024.5

1. FIRST-LINE SSRI:
   - Sertraline: Start 25-50 mg once daily.
   - Escitalopram: Start 10 mg once daily.

2. MONITORING:
   - Full effect may take 4-8 weeks.
   - Monitor suicidal ideation, especially in early treatment.

3. SAFETY GATES:
   - Avoid dual SSRI/SNRI therapy.
   - Screen for Bipolar Disorder to avoid mania induction.
