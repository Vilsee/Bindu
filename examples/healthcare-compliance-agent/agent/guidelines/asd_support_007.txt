TITLE: Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) Support Protocols
ID: ASD_SUPPORT_007
VERIFICATION: 2024.7

1. EARLY INTERVENTION:
   - Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) or similar structured behavioral therapies.
   - Speech and Occupational Therapy referrals.

2. PHARMACOLOGICAL SUPPORT (for associated symptoms):
   - Irritability/Agitation: Risperidone or Aripiprazole (FDA approved for ASD-related irritability).
   - Hyperactivity: See ADHD_MGMT_006.

3. SAFETY GATES:
   - Comprehensive metabolic monitoring if using antipsychotics.
   - Family-centered support and caregiver burnout screening.
