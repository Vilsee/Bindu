TITLE: Intellectual Disabilities & Genetic Syndromes (Down Syndrome, Williams, DiGeorge, Prader-Willi, FASD)

1. DEVELOPMENTAL SUPPORT
   - Early Intervention (EI): Speech, Physical, and Occupational therapy.
   - Prader-Willi: Weight management and rigorous food access control.
   - Williams Syndrome: Cardiac monitoring (SVAS) and musical/auditory therapy.
   - DiGeorge (22q11.2): Immunological and endocrinology screening (Calcium levels).

2. COGNITIVE ACCESSIBILITY
   - Low-arousal communication styles.
   - Visual scheduling and pictograms for task management.

3. SAFETY GATES
   - Pediatric-specific safety gates for diagnostic thresholds.
   - Caregiver-role verification for data privacy.
   - Multi-system monitoring (Genetic syndromes often involve heart/kidney/brain).
