TITLE: Asthma Management Protocol (Adults)
ID: ASTHMA_MGMT_003
VERIFICATION: 2024.3

1. MAINTENANCE THERAPY:
   - First line: Low-dose Inhaled Corticosteroid (ICS) - Formoterol as needed (MART therapy).
   - Alternative: Daily low-dose ICS plus SABA (Albuterol) as needed.

2. DOSING:
   - Budesonide/Formoterol: 160/4.5 mcg, 1-2 inhalations as needed for symptoms.
   - Fluticasone: 110 mcg, 2 inhalations twice daily.

3. SAFETY GATES:
   - Monitor Peak Flow.
   - If SABA used >2 days/week, escalate therapy.
