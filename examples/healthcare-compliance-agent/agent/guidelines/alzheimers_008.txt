TITLE: Alzheimer's Disease Symptom Management
ID: ALZHEIMERS_008
VERIFICATION: 2024.8

1. CHOLINESTERASE INHIBITORS (Mild to Moderate):
   - Donepezil: Start 5 mg once daily.
   - Rivastigmine: 1.5 mg twice daily with food.

2. NMDA RECEPTOR ANTAGONISTS (Moderate to Severe):
   - Memantine: Start 5 mg once daily; titrate to 10 mg twice daily.

3. SAFETY GATES:
   - Monitor for bradycardia and syncope.
   - Cognitive assessment (MMSE/MoCA) every 6-12 months.
   - Caregiver education on safety and wandering.
